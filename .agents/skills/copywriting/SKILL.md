# Copywriting Skill

## Objective
Write premium, founder-focused copy that sounds decisive, outcome-driven, and AI-native.

## Core Positioning
- Yuvabe is an AI-first product design, engineering, and growth partner.
- We help startups move from idea to traction, then to scale.
- We combine product + engineering + digital marketing in one execution team.
- In a world where building is no longer the main constraint, knowing what to build is the real advantage.
- Position Yuvabe as the team that helps founders discover, prioritize, and iterate toward outcomes.

## Voice
- Short, direct, confident.
- Expert but not noisy.
- Outcome-first, no agency fluff.

## Rules
1. Lead with the founder outcome, not internal process.
2. Keep hero copy tight:
   - Headline: 4-8 words preferred.
   - Subcopy: 1-2 short sentences.
3. Every section headline must communicate business value.
4. Use active verbs: build, launch, scale, convert, automate, ship.
5. Replace vague words with concrete value language.
6. Avoid buzzword stacking and generic claims.
7. Reinforce this narrative: "we do not just build fast; we help you build the right thing, then iterate to traction."

## AI-first DNA Requirements
- Mention AI-first DNA in hero or immediately below hero.
- Frame AI as practical leverage:
  - faster iteration
  - smarter automation
  - stronger growth economics
- Do not frame AI as novelty.

## CTA Guidelines
- Primary CTA should be action + audience intent:
  - `Book a Founder Call`
  - `View Startup Work`
  - `Start Your Build`
- Secondary CTA should reduce friction:
  - `How We Work`
  - `See Case Studies`

## Quality Checklist
Before finalizing copy:
1. Can a founder understand the value in under 5 seconds?
2. Is the message specific to startups (not generic B2B)?
3. Is AI-first positioning explicit and credible?
4. Are CTAs clear, distinct, and conversion-oriented?
5. Is every sentence necessary?
